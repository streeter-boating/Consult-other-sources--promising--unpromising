Mark's Savory Oats

recipe breakfast savory
March 9, 2022


Ingredients

  • 1 cup Red Mill extra thick rolled oats
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup nut milk
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp miso
  • Kimchi
  • Toasted sesame
  • Avocado (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring the oats to a boil.
  2. Once boiling. Reduce to low and cover.
  3. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until creamy
  4. Stir in miso
  5. Serve with kimchi, pickles, and a drizzle of toasted sesame oil. Avocado optional.

Mock Egg Salad

recipe lunch
March 24, 2022


Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 1/4 tsb black pepper
  • 1/4 tsb tumeric
  • 1/4 tsb dijon mustard
  • 1 tsb salt
  • 1 pickle
  • 1 bunch of micro chives
  • 3 tbsp of parsley
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1 tbsp roasted tahini
  • 1 carrot

Instructions

  1. Finely dice parsley, chives, pickle, celery, and grate carrot.
  2. Crumble tofu.
  3. Mix everything together until well distributed.
  4. Serve cold over toasted sourdough.
